Top Visited Places in the World

Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. It attracts millions of visitors each year with its stunning architecture and panoramic views of Paris.

Fun Fact: The Eiffel Tower was initially met with criticism when it was built in 1889, but it has become the most visited paid monument in the world.

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications built to protect Chinese states from invasions. It stretches over 13,000 miles and is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Fun Fact: The Great Wall is not a single continuous wall but a series of walls and fortifications built over several dynasties.

Colosseum, Rome

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater in the center of Rome, Italy. It is one of the greatest works of Roman engineering and architecture, and once hosted gladiator contests.

Fun Fact: The Colosseum could hold up to 80,000 spectators, and it hosted both combat and mock naval battles.

Fun Fact: Santorini is the remnant of an enormous volcanic eruption that shaped the island's unique landscape.
